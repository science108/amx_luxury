Our Story

Why AMX Luxury?
A Personal Mission

Hi, I'm Anna Marysia.

For years, watching the beauty industry, I felt a growing unease. Collagen was everywhere — a miracle ingredient promising eternal youth. But no one was talking about the fact that this collagen means animal suffering, a fishing industry that I — a vegetarian since childhood — simply cannot accept.

My followers, many of you from India, kept writing to me: "Anna, how do we care for our skin while respecting life?" I searched for an alternative that wouldn't just be an "eco-label," but real technology.

I went back to my roots. My father has been running a family supplement workshop for years, where ethics always came before profit. We sat down together in the lab. I wanted to create something that doesn't imitate animal collagen, but activates your own body to produce it.

That's how the AMX Luxury duo was born. These aren't just products. They are my father's legacy and my promise to you: 100% plant power, 0% suffering, 100% results.

01

The Search

Anna Marysia had been a vegetarian her entire life. As a wellness influencer, she watched the world embrace collagen — yet every product she found was derived from fish, bovine, or other animal sources. For someone who had never compromised her values, this was simply not an option.

"I couldn't ask my community to put something into their bodies that contradicts everything we believe in."
— Anna Marysia
02

A Shared Belief

Her audience — many from India and across Asia — shares a deep conviction: wellness should never come at the cost of another living being. They trusted Anna because she never faked it. She needed a solution that was as authentic as she was.

03

Family Roots

What many don't know is that Anna's father had been running an ethical supplement company for years — a quiet family business built on the belief that effective formulas and compassion can coexist. No animal testing. No animal-derived ingredients. Just science and integrity.

"My father proved that you don't have to harm to heal. I grew up watching him choose the harder, kinder path — every single time."
— Anna Marysia
04

AMX Luxury Is Born

Father and daughter joined forces. Combining his decades of formulation expertise with her understanding of what modern, conscious consumers truly need, they created AMX Luxury — a line of lab-proven, plant-based products that deliver real results without a single compromise.
